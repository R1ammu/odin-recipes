Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asperagus

Prep Time Total Time Servings Nutritional value
5 min 20 min 4-6 Servings 400 cal

Description

Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus is a delightful dish featuring succulent shrimp saut√©ed in a rich garlic butter sauce alongside tender, crisp asparagus spears. The shrimp are juicy and flavorful, absorbing the aromatic garlic and buttery goodness, while the asparagus adds a fresh, slightly crunchy contrast. This combination creates a harmonious blend of savory and fresh flavors, making it a perfect, elegant meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches asperagus, tough ends trimmed
  • 6tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2lb. medium shrimp, peeled, deveined
  • 5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp. onion powder
  • 2tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes
  • Kosher salt
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Add asparagus; season with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until asparagus are tender but still crisp, 8 to 10 minutes. Transfer to a plate.

  2. In same skillet over medium heat, cook butter, garlic, onion powder, red pepper, and remaining 1 tablespoon oil until butter is melted. Add shrimp; season with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Cook until shrimp are pink and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side. Remove from heat and stir in asparagus. Add lemon zest, lemon juice, and parsley and toss to combine.
